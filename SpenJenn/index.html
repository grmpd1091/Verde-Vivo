Who Is Spencer Jennings

Built in the Gym.
Proven on the Court.

"I build players who can think the game, not just play it."

Spencer grew up in Michigan, played Division I basketball at Wake Forest, and spent nine years competing professionally in Japan. He's a 3x3.EXE champion who's been on the FIBA World Tour — and he's been coaching in Tokyo throughout.

Every session is built around your actual game. No templates, no wasted reps — just focused work on what you actually need to improve.
